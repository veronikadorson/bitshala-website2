01

So, what actually is Bitshala?
and where do you fit in?

Bitshala is a collective — clubs, cohorts, a paid fellowship — built around helping people contribute to Bitcoin FOSS. Whether you've never opened a terminal or you ship code for a living, there's a door cut for you. Pick yours.

Path A — the curious

I'm new to Bitcoin. Where do I even start?

Start with our Reading Club and Latest in Bitcoin Tech sessions. Friendly rooms, no jargon-shaming, real humans.

Take the slow path
Path B — the builder

I write code. Take me to the protocol.

Jump into the PR Review Club, BITDEVS, Study Cohorts and the paid Fellowship. Ship to bitcoind, LDK, BDK, Fedimint.

Take the fast path
